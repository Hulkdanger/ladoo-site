The Language track

From words to sentences

Start with greetings and build toward real conversation, one short lesson at a time.

  • Bite-size lessons that fit into a commute or a chai break.
  • A word bank that grows with every lesson you finish.
  • Streaks and gems that keep you coming back without pressure.

The Gurmukhi script

From zero to reading

A dedicated track for the script itself. Master every letter and vowel, then trace them with your finger until they feel like home.

  • Every letter and vowel, taught in a careful order.
  • Finger tracing so your hand learns the shapes too.
  • No prior reading ability assumed, ever.

Speaking practice

Hear it, then say it

Every lesson makes you speak out loud. Ladoo listens and checks your pronunciation word by word, so you build a voice, not just a vocabulary.

  • Native-quality audio for every word and sentence.
  • Word-by-word feedback on what you said.
  • A gentle pace that never punishes a wobbly first try.

Read in context

Short stories, real context

Mornings in the village, kite festivals, langar at the gurdwara. Read Punjabi the way it is actually used, with narration and translations a tap away.

  • Beginner to intermediate reads, three to five minutes each.
  • Full narration recorded line by line.
  • Culture built in, not bolted on.

Your AI speaking tutor

Talk it out with Bhaji

Bhaji chats with you in Punjabi, drills your current lessons, and remembers your past mistakes. Type or speak, and ask for a translation whenever you need one.

  • Real conversation practice about everyday scenarios.
  • Drills built from your mistakes, not a generic script.
  • Voice or text, whichever feels comfortable today.
