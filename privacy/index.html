Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026  ·  Effective: June 24, 2026

Ladoo is an app for learning Punjabi, including a conversational AI tutor ("Bhaji"). This policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, who we share it with, how long we keep it, and the choices and rights you have.

The short version. We collect the information needed to run your account, teach you Punjabi, power the AI tutor, and process subscriptions. To transcribe your speech and generate tutor replies, we send your voice and messages to Google's speech and AI services. We do not sell your personal information, we do not show ads, and we do not track you across other apps or websites. You can delete your account and all associated data from within the app at any time.

1. Who we are

Ladoo (the "App") is operated by Yuvraj Dhamija, an individual developer ("we," "us," or "our"). We are the controller responsible for your personal information as described in this policy.

If you have any questions, requests, or complaints about your privacy, contact us at LadooSupport@gmail.com.

This policy applies to the Ladoo mobile app and its supporting backend services. It does not apply to third-party services we link to or rely on, each of which has its own privacy policy (see Third-party services).

2. Information we collect

We collect the categories of information below. We only collect what we need to operate the App, and several features work without an account in a limited offline mode.

a. Account & identity information

When you create an account, we collect your email address and a unique account identifier. Depending on how you sign in:

  • Email & password (we store your email and a securely hashed password, handled by our authentication provider, Supabase; we never see your plaintext password).
  • Sign in with Google (we receive your email address, name, and basic profile information from Google).
  • Sign in with Apple (we receive your Apple account identifier and, on first sign-in, optionally your name and an email address, which may be Apple's private relay address if you choose to hide your email).

You may also set or edit a display name / username shown in the App.

b. Voice & audio

The voice tutor lets you speak Punjabi using your device microphone. When you record a turn, the audio is captured to a temporary file on your device, sent securely to our backend, and forwarded to Google Cloud Speech-to-Text for transcription. We do not store your raw audio recordings, the temporary file is discarded after the turn is processed. The resulting transcript (the text of what you said) is saved as part of your tutor chat history so you can review your conversations.

c. AI tutor content

When you chat with the AI tutor, we collect and store the messages you send and receive (in Gurmukhi script, romanization, and English), any corrections the tutor provides, and contextual learning data used to personalize tutoring (such as your current lesson focus, target vocabulary, and recent mistakes). To generate replies, this content is sent to Google's AI service (see Section 4).

d. Learning & progress data

We store your learning activity so your progress syncs across sessions, including: lessons completed and scores, words learned, experience points (XP), daily streaks, "hearts," "gems," your saved "mistake bank" (questions you answered incorrectly, kept for targeted practice), and settings such as your daily goal, learning reason, sound, and romanization preferences.

e. Subscriptions & purchases

If you purchase a Ladoo Pro subscription, the transaction is processed by Apple's App Store, we never receive or store your full payment card details. To verify and activate your subscription, we store a purchase record including the product purchased, Apple's transaction identifier, your subscription/trial status and expiry date, and the verification response returned by Apple.

f. Usage & analytics events

We log a limited set of product events to understand how features are used and to improve the App, for example when a lesson is started or completed, a feature such as the AI tutor or flashcards is opened, a screen is viewed, a subscription screen is viewed, a call-to-action is tapped, a purchase is started or completed, a free trial begins, or a free-tier limit is reached. These events are keyed to your account identifier (not your name or email) and are stored in our own backend and with our product-analytics provider, PostHog, which we use to measure retention and feature usage. Alongside each event, PostHog automatically records basic technical context (such as device and operating-system type, app version, and a session identifier) and derives an approximate location (such as country, region, and city) from your IP address; we use this only for product analytics, never for advertising. We have not enabled session recording or screen replay, so PostHog does not capture video-style recordings of your screen or your taps; we only log the discrete events described above. We do not use third-party advertising or cross-app tracking analytics (such as ad networks), and we do not track you across other apps or websites.

g. Diagnostics & crash data

To keep the App stable, we may collect crash reports and error diagnostics through Sentry. These include technical details such as error messages, stack traces, and basic device/app context. We have configured Sentry not to send default personally identifying information (for example, your IP address is not sent), and we do not send your conversations or account email to Sentry.

h. Device & technical information

When the App communicates with our backend, we and our service providers may process technical information necessary to deliver the service, such as app version, device/OS type, and network information (including your IP address, which is processed transiently for security, rate-limiting, and abuse prevention).

i. Communications & feedback

If you use the in-app "Contact us" form, we collect the email address and message you provide so we can respond and improve the App. If you email us directly, we receive whatever information you include.

j. Information stored only on your device

Some information stays on your device and is not transmitted to us, including onboarding status, your daily-goal and learning-reason selections, your AI-consent choice, your romanization preference, and a local cache of completed lessons used for offline access.

3. How we use information

We use the information described above to:

  • Create and manage your account and authenticate you;
  • Provide core learning features and sync your progress across sessions and devices;
  • Transcribe your speech and generate AI tutor responses;
  • Personalize tutoring based on your lessons, vocabulary, and past mistakes;
  • Process, verify, and manage subscriptions and free trials;
  • Send you service and reminder communications (such as a trial-ending email);
  • Respond to feedback, support requests, and messages you send us;
  • Maintain reliability, debug crashes, and improve the App;
  • Protect the service through rate-limiting, fraud prevention, and abuse detection; and
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

4. AI tutor & speech processing

The AI tutor and voice features rely on Google's cloud AI and speech services:

  • Speech-to-text: your recorded audio is sent to Google Cloud Speech-to-Text to convert it into text.
  • Tutor responses: your messages, transcripts, and learning context are sent to Google's Gemini API (Google AI / Google Cloud) to generate the tutor's replies.
  • Text-to-speech: Punjabi text is sent to Google Cloud Text-to-Speech to produce spoken audio.

Please don't share sensitive personal information in the chat. Because tutor messages and voice are processed by a third-party AI service to generate responses, avoid sending sensitive details (such as financial information, government identifiers, or health information). Before you first use the AI tutor, we ask for your explicit consent to this processing, and we record the date you consented.

Your use of these features is also subject to Google's privacy terms, linked in the next section.

6. How information is shared

We share personal information only in these limited circumstances:

  • With the service providers above, who process data on our behalf to deliver the App;
  • With Apple, as part of processing and verifying your subscription;
  • For legal reasons, if required to comply with applicable law, legal process, or a lawful government request, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of our users or the public; and
  • In a business transfer, if the App or its assets are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale, in which case we will notify you of any change in control of your information.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it for cross-context behavioral advertising.

7. International data transfers

We and our service providers operate primarily in the United States, and our providers may process data in the U.S. and other countries. If you access the App from outside these regions (for example, from the EEA or UK), your information may be transferred to and processed in countries whose data-protection laws differ from those in your country. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards (such as the providers' standard contractual clauses) for these transfers.

8. Data retention

We keep personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the App, then delete or anonymize it, except where a longer period is required for legal, security, or accounting reasons. Specifically:

  • Voice recordings: not retained (discarded immediately after transcription).
  • Tutor chat history and learning progress: kept until you delete the conversation or your account.
  • Mistake bank: automatically purged after about 30 days.
  • Subscription records: retained as long as needed for entitlement and record-keeping.
  • Analytics events: retained in aggregate/operational form to improve the App.
  • Crash diagnostics (Sentry): retained per Sentry's default retention (generally around 90 days).

When you delete your account, your associated records are removed from our active systems (see Your rights & choices).

9. Your rights & choices

You have control over your information:

  • Delete your account and data. You can permanently delete your account from within the App. This removes your profile, progress, tutor conversations, mistake bank, analytics, subscription records, and feedback from our active systems.
  • Access & correction. You can view and edit much of your profile in the App, and you may contact us to request access to or correction of your personal information.
  • Microphone. Voice features only work if you grant microphone permission. You can revoke it anytime in your device settings; the rest of the App will still work.
  • Notifications. You can enable or disable reminder notifications in the App or your device settings.
  • Emails. Reminder emails include a one-click unsubscribe link, and you can opt out of email reminders in the App.
  • AI tutor. The AI tutor is optional and requires your consent before any data is sent for processing.

To make any privacy request, email LadooSupport@gmail.com. We will respond within the timeframe required by applicable law and may need to verify your identity before acting on certain requests. We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights.
